# High Fiber Gluten Free Bread

Source: hallysdailylife

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup psyllium husk
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 cup water
- 2 tsp active dry yeast
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1/2 cup warm water
- 2 cups GF baking flour
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp kosher salt

## Directions

- Soak psyllium, vinegar, water in bowl to form gel
- Soak yeast, honey, water in bowl and let bloom
- Mix flour, baking powder, salt in medium bowl
- Mix the two wet bowls into the dry
- Knead dough for 5 mins and let rest for 1-2 hours
- Heat oven to 400 F
- Transfer dough to oiled & floured bread pan
- Bake at 400 F for 40-60 mins until done
- Let cool 10 mins before serving
