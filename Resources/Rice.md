# Rice

## White Rice

- Rinse the rice
- Use 1.5:1 ratio of water:rice in a pot
- Bring to boil over med-high heat
- Turn to low, cover and simmer for 20 mins
- Remove from heat for 5 minutes before fluffing with fork

Optional: use broth instead of water for more flavor

## Brown Rice

- Same as white rice, but use 2:1 water:rice ratio
