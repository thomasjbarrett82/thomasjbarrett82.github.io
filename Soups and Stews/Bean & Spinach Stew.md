# Bean & Spinach Stew

Source: @marcus.olaoire on Instagram

## Ingredients

- EVOO
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 stalks celery, diced
- 2 large carrots, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- Kosher salt & pepper
- 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 3 cans butter beans
- 2 cups white wine
- 1 packet gravy mix
- 1 tbsp. bouillon
- 1 lb. cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 lb. fresh spinach, washed
- fresh parsley
- lemon juice

## Prep

- Heat EVOO in large pan over med heat

## Directions

- Add mirepoix and cook for 5 minutes until soft
- Add garlic, salt, pepper, seasoning for 1 minute
- Add beans, wine, gravy mix, bouillon
- Bring to low boil
- Turn to low and cover to simmer for 30 minutes
- Add tomatoes & spinach, cooked until spinach is wilted
- Add parsley and lemon juice to taste
