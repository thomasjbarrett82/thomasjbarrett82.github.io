# Chicken & Wild Rice Soup

Source: Serious Eats

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp oil
- 2-3 lbs chicken thighs
- 4 tbsp butter
- 4 celery stalks, chopped
- 1 large carrot, sliced
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 tbsp kosher salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp thyme (OR 1 tbsp fresh thyme)
- 1/4 cup GF flour
- 3 quarts chicken stock (homemade is best)
- 1 cup uncooked wild rice
- 1/8 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1 tsp lemon zest + 2 tbsp lemon juice

## Prep

- Heat large Dutch oven over medium heat

## Directions

- Add oil to pot and brown chicken thighs
- Remove to plate, shred, and set aside
  - Remove bones if present
- Melt butter in pot
- Add celery, carrot, onion, salt, pepper
- Cook until softened
- Add garlic & thyme for 1 minute
- Add flour and stir until starting to stick
- Pour in stock and stir until flour is fully incorporated
- Add rice and baking soda
- Bring to boil over med-high heat
- Reduce heat to simmer and add chicken back in
- Cover and cook for 40 minutes
- Stir in heavy cream and lemon
- Garnish with some fresh parsley or celery leaves
