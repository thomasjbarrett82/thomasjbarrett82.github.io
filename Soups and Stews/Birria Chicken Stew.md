# Birria Chicken Stew

Source: Tom

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs chicken thighs
- birria seasoning for 2 lbs meat
- 1 bag frozen peppers & onion
- 1 bag frozen corn
- 2 cans seasoned black beans
- 2 cans Rotel
- 1 tbsp kosher salt
- Tostadas, cheese, cilantro, sour cream, diced avocado for serving

## Directions

- Place chicken & seasoning in crockpot
- Cook on high for 2-3 hours until tender
- Remove any bones and shred the meat
- Add veggies, beans, Rotel, salt
- Cook on high for another 2 hours
- Serve with toppings
