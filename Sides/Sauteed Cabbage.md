# Sauteed Cabbage

Source: @whatinthechef on Instagram

## Ingredients

- EVOO
- 1 onion, sliced
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 head cabbage, thinly sliced
- OPTIONAL: 1 lb. fresh spinach
- 1 tbsp. kosher salt
- 2 tbsp. garam masala
- 1/2 cup green chilis
- 1/2 cup water
- Naan, pita, or rice for a base

## Prep

- Heat EVOO in large pan over med heat

## Directions

- Add onion, garlic and cook 3-5 mins until soft
- Add cabbage and seasonings to pan
- Stir until starting to soften and wilt
- Add water, turn to low, and cover pan
- Cook for 15-20 minutes
- Top with any fresh herbs and serve over the base
