# Mashed Pro-tatoes

## Ingredients

- 5 lbs. russet potatoes, peeled & chunked
- 1 tbsp. bouillon
- water
- 1.5 cups cottage cheese
- 1 tsp. garlic salt
- 1 tsp. kosher salt
- 4 tbsp. butter
- 1 egg

## Directions

- Place potatoes in pot
- Add water to cover by 1"
- Stir in the bouillon
- Cover with lid and set on high
- Add cottage cheese and salts to blender
- Boil until potatoes are fork tender
- Before potatoes are finished, add 1 cup of water to blender
- Blend cheese, water, salts
- Drain potatoes and return to pot
- Add cheese mix, butter, and egg to pot
- Mash or blend everything together before serving
