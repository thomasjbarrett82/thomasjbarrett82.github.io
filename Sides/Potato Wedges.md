# Potato Wedges

## Ingredients

- 2 lb. Yukon or Gold potatoes, cut into wedges
- 1+1 tbsp EVOO
- 1 packet Ranch seasoning (or other flavors)

## Directions

- Pat wedges dry and toss with 1 tbsp EVOO
- Add to 400 F air fryer for 20 mins
- Remove potatoes and toss with EVOO and ranch
- Return to fryer for 8-10 minutes until golden brown
