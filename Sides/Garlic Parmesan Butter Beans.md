# Garlic Parmesan Butter Beans

Source: Tom

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp butter
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 cup broth
- 4 oz cream cheese (room temperature)
- 2 15 oz cans butter beans, drained and rinsed
- 1/2 cup parmesan

## Prep

- Heat large pan over medium heat

## Directions

- Melt butter and add seasonings for 1 minute
- Stir in broth and cream cheese until melted
- Stir in beans and simmer uncovered for 10-15 minutes
- Stir in parmesan and serve warm
