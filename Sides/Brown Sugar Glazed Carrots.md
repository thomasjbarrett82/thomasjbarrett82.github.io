# Brown Sugar Glazed Carrots

Source: Tom

## Ingredients

- 2 lb carrots
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 tsp garlic salt
- 2 tbsp butter, melted
- 1/4 tsp black pepper

## Prep

- Heat air fryer or roaster oven to 400 F

## Directions

- Cut carrots to similar size pieces
- Toss with remaining ingredients in bowl
- Spread on greased pan
- Roast 15 mins
- Toss and roast another 10 mins
- Toss to cover with glaze and serve warm
