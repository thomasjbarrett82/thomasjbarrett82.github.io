# Protein Waffles

Source: Instagram

## Ingredients

- 40 g rolled oats
- 60 g gluten free flour
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1 scoop protein powder
- 1 egg
- 120+ ml skim milk

## Prep

- Heat waffle iron

## Directions

- Blend all ingredients until smooth
- Add more milk, if needed, to get a pourable consistency
- Cook batter in waffle iron
- Makes ~4-5 mini waffles
