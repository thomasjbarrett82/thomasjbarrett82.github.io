# Black Bean Brownies (GF)

Source: @bdylanhollis

## Ingredients

- 15 ounce can black beans, drained and rinsed
- 2 tbsp. cocoa powder
- 3/4 cup quick oats
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1/2 cup honey
- 3 tbsp. sugar
- 1/4 cup butter, melted
- 1 tsp. vanilla extract
- 1/2 tsp. baking powder
- 1 egg
- 2 tsp psyllium husk powder
- 2/3 cup chocolate chips
- 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

## Prep

- Heat oven to 350 F
- Grease an 8x8 pan OR brownie pan
  - OR line with parchment paper

## Directions

- Add all ingredients except chocolate chips & walnuts to food processor
- Blend until smooth
- Stir in chocolate chips & walnuts
- Pour into pan
- Bake for 25-35 minutes
- Let cool at least 10 minutes before serving
  - Optionally sprinkle with some more chocolate chips before it cools
