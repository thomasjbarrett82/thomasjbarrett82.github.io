# Snickers Salad

Source: Instagram

## Ingredients

- 3 thinly sliced apples
- 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 1/4 cup nut butter
- 1/4 cup caramel sauce
- 1/4 cup chocolate chips
- 1/4 cup chopped nuts

## Directions

- Mix everything in a large bowl
- Chill for at least 1 hour before serving
