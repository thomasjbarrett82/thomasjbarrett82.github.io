# Butter Chicken

Source: Two Sleevers

## Ingredients

- 1+1 tbsp. EVOO
- 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- 14 oz can diced tomatoes
- 4-5 cloves garlic, sliced
- 2 tsp ginger, minced
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/4 cup butter, cubed
- 1/4 cup heavy cream or Greek yogurt
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 bag frozen broccoli, thawed

## Prep

- Heat large pot over medium heat

## Directions

- Add 1 tbsp EVOO and brown chicken in pot
- Remove the chicken to a plate for the next step
- Add 1 tbsp EVOO and spices to the pot
- Cook for 1 minute until fragrant
- Add tomatoes to the pot
- Add the chicken back to the pot on top of the tomatoes
- Turn down heat to low-medium and cover
- Cook for 20-30 minutes until chicken is cooked through
- Remove chicken and set aside to rest
- Add butter, cream, garam masala to sauce
- Blend sauce with an immersion blender, or mash it
- Rough chop the chicken and add it back to the sauce
- Add broccoli and let sit for a few mins to warm up
- Serve over rice or veggies
