# Sweet Potato Chickpea Bowl

Source: PlantYou

## Ingredients

- 2 sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 15 oz can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 1 tbsp EVOO
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
- 1 bag coleslaw mix
- 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp honey (hot honey also good)
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Prep

- Heat convection oven to 400 F

## Directions

- Toss potatoes, chickpeas, EVOO, paprika, garlic, BBQ in medium bowl
- Spread on pan and roast for 20 mins until potatoes are softened
- Meanwhile mix remaining ingredients in medium bowl
- Stir in potatoes & chickpeas once finished cooking
- Divide between 2 large bowls to serve
