# Black Bean Tacos

Source: Instagram

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp EVOO
- 1 onion, diced
- 1/2 cup carrot, shredded
- 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 cans black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 cup stock
- S&P to taste

## Prep

- Heat large pan over medium heat and add EVOO

## Directions

- Saute onion and carrots for 5 mins
- Add garlic for 1 min
- Add spices & tomato paste for 1 min
- Add beans & stock
- Bring to boil and cook for 5 mins
- Roughly mash and serve with taco shells and toppings
