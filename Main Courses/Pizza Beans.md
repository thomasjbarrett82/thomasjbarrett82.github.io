# Pizza Beans

Source: Subversive Table

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp EVOO
- 1 small onion, minced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can mushrooms, drained
- 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tsp chili flakes
- 28 oz pizza or pasta sauce
- 28 oz canned white beans, drained and rinsed
- 2 cups mozzarella cheese
- Toppings (olives, picled veggies, pepperoni, etc.)
- Parmesan

## Prep

- Heat large CI skillet over med heat
- Preheat oven to 400 F

## Directions

- Saute onion in EVOO until softened
- Add garlic and mushrooms for a few minutes
- Add seasonings until fragrant
- Add sauce and beans and simmer for 5-10 minutes
- Cover with mozzarella and toppings
- Move to oven for ~30 mins until bubbling and golden brown
- Remove and add Parmesan and fresh herbs, if desired
- Let cool 5 mins before serving
