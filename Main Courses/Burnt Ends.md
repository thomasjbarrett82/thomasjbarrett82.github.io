# Burnt Ends

Source: Hey Grill Hey

## Ingredients

- 3-6 lb brisket point, trimmed from whole
- 4 tbsp spice rub
  - 1 tbsp each: salt, pepper, garlic powder, smoked paprika
- 1 cup beef stock
- charcoal and wood chunks
- disposable aluminum pan
- 1 cup BBQ sauce
- 1/2 cup brown sugar

## Prep

- Season brisket with rub and refrigerate for 12-24 hours
- Heat smoker to 225 F using snake method

## Directions

- Position thermometer in middle of brisket
- Add brisket to grate over pan, fat side up
- Cover and adjust until temp is 225-275 F
- Smoke until brisket reaches 160-170 F
  - 6-10 hours
  - Spritz with beef stock every hour
- Remove brisket and wrap with single parchment and double foil
- Return to grill, fat side up, and continue until 195-200 F
  - 2-4 hours
  - Alternately, move to 225 F oven to finish cooking
- Remove brisket to cutting board
- Unwrap and drain any liquid into pan
- Cut into 1.5 inch cubes
- Move cubes and any juice to pan and toss with BBQ sauce & sugar
- Move pan back to smoker
- Continue at 225 F for 1-2 hours until BBQ starts caramelizing
- Remove and let rest for 10 minutes before serving
