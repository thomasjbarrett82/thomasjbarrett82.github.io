# Meatloaf

Source: Pillsbury Cookbook

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. ground beef
- 1/2 cup ketchup
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs
- 1 tsp. Italian seasoning
- 1 tsp. garlic salt
- 1 tsp. onion powder
- Black pepper to taste

## Prep

- Heat oven to 350 F

## Directions

- Mix everything in a large bowl
- Form into a loaf in a small baking dish
- Bake for 60 minutes until internal temp is 150 F
- Remove and let stand for 5 minutes
- Spread more ketchup over the top for decoration, if desired
