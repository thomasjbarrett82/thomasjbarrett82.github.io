# Butter Chicken (Instant Pot)

Source: Two Sleevers

## Ingredients

- 1+1 tbsp EVOO
- 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- 14 oz can diced tomatoes
- 4-5 cloves garlic, sliced
- 2 tsp ginger, minced
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/4 cup butter, cubed
- 1/4 cup heavy cream or Greek yogurt
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 bag frozen broccoli, thawed

## Directions

- Heat IP on saute
- Add 1 tbsp EVOO and brown the chicken
- Remove chicken and set to the side
- Add 1 tbsp EVOO and spices, cook until fragrant
- Stir in tomatoes and put chicken thighs on top
- Close lid and cook on high pressure for 10 mins
- Natural release for 10 mins before opening
- Remove chicken and set aside
- Add butter, cream, garam masala to sauce
- Blend sauce with an immersion blender
- Rough chop chicken and add it back to the sauce
- Add broccoli and let sit for a few mins to warm up
- Serve over rice or veggies
