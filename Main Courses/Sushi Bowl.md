# Sushi Bowl

Source: Instagram

## Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked white rice (brown for more fiber)
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 12 oz smoked salmon
- Salt & pepper to taste
- Sesame seeds
- Optional: sushi ingredients like diced cucumber, kimchi, etc.

## Prep

- Toss avocado with lemon juice to keep fresh

## Directions

- Split rice into 2 bowls
- Arrange salmon, avocado, other ingredients over rice
- Sprinkle with S&P, sesame seeds
- Serve with soy sauce
