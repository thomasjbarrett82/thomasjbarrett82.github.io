# Smoked Rack of Pork

## Ingredients

- 1 rack of pork
- BBQ spice rub
- BBQ sauce (optional)

## Prep

- Heat smoker to 225 F
- Optional: cover with rub and let stand for 8 hours in fridge

## Directions

- Smother spice rub all over rack
- Place on grill and smoke for 4-5 hours until internal temp is 140 F
- Occasionally baste with BBQ sauce in the last hour of cooking
- Remove and let rest for at least 10 minutes
- Optional: broil for 5 minutes to give a nice crust
- Slice between ribs to serve
